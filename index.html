ABOUT

Finding a missing person can be a difficult task, but if you know where to look, it is often much easier to find a missing person.

The directory of missing people uses Openchain to keep a track of each entry and make it transparent.

For those who are not aware, Openchain is a Blockchain technology. but it directly links transactions to one another instead of having to use blocks.

This makes our directory faster.

Psst. Our directory is scalable too, that is, it is almost endless, and free.
